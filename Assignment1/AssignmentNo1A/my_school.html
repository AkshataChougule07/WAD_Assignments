Welcome to S.V.C.S School

The location of S V C S High School is Solapur South, Solapur. It is a State Board school, with a team of 27 dedicated and professional faculties, which are here to ensure that the children get the most from their education. 1959 saw S V C S High School being launched. This school's primary medium of instruction is Marathi and the student teacher ratio is 42:1. The school tries to provide best learning environment. The school provides education to students from class 6 to 12. This school's student strength is approximately 1147. This school's library has 4483 books. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too.
